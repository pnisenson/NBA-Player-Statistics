About Data Types

Season entered is the spring of the season (e.g. Start Year 1985 is the 1984-85 season). Data types may be unavailable or incomplete for certain years. For example, in 1950 minutes played and games started were not tracked. The NBA began counting blocks in 1973-74 and added a three-point line in 1979-80, respectively. Basketball-Reference maintains each data type beginning in the following years:

  • Totals/Per Game/Per 36 Mins: 1950
  • Per 100 Possesions: 1974
  • Play-by-Play/Shooting: 1997
